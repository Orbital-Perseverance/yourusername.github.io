ORBITAL

Simulation-driven aerospace systems

Mission

Orbital develops physics-based aerospace simulations focused on autonomous flight, guidance, and control systems where real-world testing is limited by cost and regulation.

What We Build

• Ascent trajectory simulations
• Descent and landing control algorithms
• Throttle and gimbal control logic
• Guidance, Navigation & Control (GNC)
• Failure analysis and edge-case testing

Approach

Orbital follows a simulation-first development process, validating control logic and system behavior in virtual environments before any hardware implementation.
